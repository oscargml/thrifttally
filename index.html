FAQ

Before you trust the numbers

How accurate are these fee estimates?

They're built from each marketplace's published seller rate card and are accurate for most standard listings. Some categories carry special rates (eBay's business & industrial tier, promoted-listing add-ons, Etsy Plus subscriptions), so treat this as a planning estimate and confirm the exact number in your seller dashboard before you price an item.

Does the calculator include the cost of shipping?

You can enter what you personally pay to ship an item, and it's subtracted from your payout. It does not simulate what a buyer pays for shipping, since that varies by carrier, weight, and platform-negotiated rates.

Do I owe taxes on resale income?

In the US, marketplaces are required to send you (and the IRS) a 1099-K once your sales cross certain thresholds, and income from reselling for profit is generally taxable regardless of whether a form is issued. This calculator doesn't estimate tax — talk to a tax professional about your specific situation.

Which platform is cheapest for vintage clothing specifically?

As of 2026, Depop charges 0% seller commission for US and UK sellers (just payment processing), which usually makes it the lowest-fee option for fashion. Etsy and eBay often net more in absolute dollars for well-described, search-friendly vintage pieces despite higher fees, because of stronger buyer intent.

How often do these fees change?

Marketplaces adjust seller fees periodically, sometimes with little notice. This page is reviewed and updated when rate cards change; the "fees last checked" date above the calculator shows when it was last verified.
